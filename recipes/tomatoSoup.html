Tomato Soup Recipe

Desciption

You are just six basic steps and 30 minutes away from enjoying the smoothest, creamiest, most tangy and delicious homemade tomato soup.

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon unsalted butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1 onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 large garlic cloves, peeled and crushed
  • 2 (28 ounce) cans whole peeled tomatoes
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • ¼ teaspoon celery seed
  • ¼ teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 pinch red pepper flakes
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Heat butter and olive oil in a large saucepan over medium-low heat.
  2. Cook onion and garlic until onion is soft and translucent, about 5 minutes.
  3. Add tomatoes, water, sugar, celery seed, oregano, red pepper flakes, salt, and pepper.
  4. Bring to a boil.
  5. Reduce heat, cover, and simmer for 15 minutes.
  6. Remove from heat and puree with an immersion blender
  7. Reheat soup until warm and season with salt and pepper.
